The Sisters' Tribe.

PICK. RISE. SOAR.

We want to empower and enable women and girls to take charge of their lives and in line
with this goal of ours, we aim to direct our resources to generate employment opportunities
for rural women, helping create awareness and debunking the stigma, and placing immense
emphasis on community-care, which will ultimately help in raising women’s self-esteem and
broaden their perspectives.

Empowerment through employment

website2 seeks to enable rural women to become independent in all spheres of life by generating and providing them
with employment opportunities. Through our initiatives, we aim to encourage women in rural areas to explore avenues
that advance economic independence, further helping them break the cycle of poverty.

Breaking the taboo

Despite being the most natural biological process, menstruation is still viewed through a lens fogged with rampant stigma and
taboo and thus, acts as a constant barrier to attaining gender equality. Our initiatives are aimed at debunking all the myths and
stigma surrounding menstrual health by spreading awareness in both men and women so they can become more informed and
self-aware and pass on this knowledge to their forthcoming generations.

Be your own friend

Women are often imposed with societal norms and expected to put their families and spouses above themselves which
propagates a sense of low self-esteem within them. By helping women and girls prioritize their menstrual wellness and hygiene,
we encourage women to take agency in their lives and be their own person first, ultimately uplifting their sense of worth and
esteem.

Community of women

They say it takes a village to raise a child and website2’s vision deeply resonates with this phrase. We strongly believe in
community-care and so, we want to build a community and close-knit network of women who are each other’s friends and
confidants helping each other by sharing their experiences and life stories.
