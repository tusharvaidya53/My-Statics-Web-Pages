Our Vision
To rejuvenate EERGIC. (Ecosystem for Entrepreneurial Resources Generating Interdependent Collaboration)
What We Do
  • Formation Of FPO / FPC, OFPO, Cooperatives, Pvt Ltd, Public Limited, Hybrid Models –Farmer Clubs, Farmer SHGs Federation, Consumer Forums/ Unions/ Companies/ Societies, Grahak Bhandar, etc comprising Farmers/ Producers, Consumers, Processors, Exporters, Buyers and allied Service Providers.
  • Validating and Connecting Above Such Players On Single Physical and Digital Platform to upgrade them to set EERGIC Standards.
  • Facilitating Financial Support To Above Such Players Using Available and Innovative Tools.
  • Facilitating Basic Infra (Human and Allied) To Above All Players By Helping To Build, Acquire, Contract, Hire, Lease) For Smooth Activities.
  • Providing Mental/Psychological Stability Via Assured Income and Insurance Tools To All Stake Holders By Mutually Collaborative Activities Of Producers, Consumers and Service Producers.
  • Training, Education, Certification of Processes and Products of Affiliates.
  • Promotion of Low Cost Farming.
Our Mission
  • To reestablish a sustainable, self sufficient, reliable system based on agriculture & villages for rural and urban entrepreneurship in organized form.
  • To create a platform for connecting rural & urban communities for traditional value based eco friendly entrepreneurial ecosystem.
